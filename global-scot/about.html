--- layout: globalscot title: What is GlobalScot? ---

GlobalScot is a diverse network of business leaders, entrepreneurs and executives with a connection to Scotland - and a strong desire to see Scottish businesses succeed locally and in the wider world.

Started by Scottish Enterprise in 2001, the network has helped transform the face of Scottish business, giving hundreds of emerging and growing companies in Scotland a head start to compete in a global marketplace.

If you're interested in harnessing new business opportunities and developing your Scottish brand internationally, GlobalScot can provide the advice you need.
