--- layout: globalscot title: What is GlobalScot? ---

GlobalScot is a diverse network of business leaders, entrepreneurs and executives with a connection to Scotland - and a strong desire to see Scottish businesses succeed locally and in the wider world.

Started by Scottish Enterprise in 2001, the network has helped transform the face of Scottish business, giving hundreds of emerging and growing companies in Scotland a head start to compete in a global marketplace.

If you're interested in harnessing new business opportunities and developing your Scottish brand internationally, GlobalScot can provide the advice you need.

What is a GlobalScot's role?

As a GlobalScot you can help Scottish companies in a variety of ways, which ultimately supports the growth of the Scottish economy.

Each year we invite business leaders excelling in their field to our esteemed network. They are chosen for their expertise and experience; their business savvy is instrumental to maintaining the GlobalScot reputation as a world-class resource for Scottish business.

I find that addressing issues in adjacent areas always makes you look at your own business challenges differently. I'm looking forward to challenging and thought-provoking engagements that will help us all develop our businesses.
