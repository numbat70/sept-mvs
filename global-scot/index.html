What is GS network?

Scots are recognised the world over for their business acumen, their entrepreneurial spirit and their drive to succeed.

Ambition, strong leadership and hunger for success are just some of the qualities which have helped Scottish businessmen and women occupy senior positions in world leading companies and organisations.

Since 2001, Scottish Enterprise’s GlobalScot initiative has tapped into this international network of business excellence, assisting hundreds of new and growing companies aiming to make their mark on the international marketplace.

Our unique service provides the next generation of Scottish business success stories with access to a powerful and influential group of experts who are keen to pass on their knowledge and expertise.

And our network has never been more active.

How can we help you?

As a GlobalScot you can help Scottish companies in many ways, which ultimately supports the growth of the Scottish economy.

GlobalScots can assist you across a number of key areas which can have a direct impact on your business growth.

GlobalScots will deliver unrivalled insight, information and intelligence on your sectors and the territories you are targeting for growth.

And you’ll have the opportunity at events in Scotland and in your target markets to make connections with global leaders and benefit from one-to-one advice sessions.

GlobalScots can provide you with the skills, knowledge and advice to ensure you embed a strong culture of leadership in your business, helping you to strengthen and motivate your management team.

GlobalScots can help with advice on how to prepare for new market entry and how best to find customers as well as identifying potential partners and suppliers.
