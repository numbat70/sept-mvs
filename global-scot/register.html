--- layout: globalscot title: Is GlobalScot for you? ---

To benefit from the network, you're likely to be an established Scottish company with serious ambitions to grow or belong to academia.

GlobalScots have a maximum impact where they can assist Scottish companies that are at a critical point in their development and seeking support with specific barriers to growth.

Get support for your business?

If your business is at an early stage of development, we would suggest you first access business support and international trade services from Scottish Enterprise and our partners:

  • Business Gateway
  • Scottish Enterprise export services
  • Scottish Enterprise support services
  • Business Scotland

How do you connect with GlobalScots?

Our worldwide network of senior business people can help you to identify new leads, develop your business and get into world markets that might otherwise be difficult to access.

If you are an established Scottish company and are ready to expand your business locally and globally, then we would like to help you to achieve your full potential.
